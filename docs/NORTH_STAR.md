# North Star

## North Star Statement
HorseSense should produce objective, reproducible horse movement/posture measurements from ordinary stable video, starting with robust pose tracking and honest confidence reporting.

## MVP Success Signal
A non-technical stakeholder can watch an overlay and read a short report, then trust that the system is measuring movement/posture consistently enough to inform next steps (camera recapture vs. model fine-tuning).

## Principles
- Objective over speculative (measure motion/pose, avoid emotion claims)
- Visual evidence first (overlay is the primary proof)
- Offline-friendly workflow (works on office laptop)
- Honest uncertainty (likelihood, jitter, failure modes always reported)
- Reusable pipeline (same CLI for sample and Jack clips)
